Digital Signature and Electronic Signature, Similar … But Different
Holland & Knight LLP
Holland & Knight LLP logo
USA November 6 2024
Click here to listen to the audio

In this episode of "A Lo Legal En Par Minutos," Partner Edwin Cortés talks with corporate attorney Anna Catalina Pérez about the intricacies of electronic signatures and digital signatures. The attorneys discuss the legal frameworks governing them in Colombia, noting the evolving significance of electronic signatures in the post-pandemic environment of increased digital interactions. The conversation sheds light on the practical implications of utilizing electronic signatures, providing valuable insights into their legal validity and related technological trends.
